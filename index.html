WHAT ARE THESE
BROKEN BONES?

Broken Bones is one of the biggest collections crawling out of the Middle East with over 10,000 cracked skeletons ready to haunt the Ethereum Blockchain.

'Not everything that dies completely fades away'

These Skeletons are rising from the dead, digging out of their graves, searching for brains to feed on and battling with other hungry creatures who try to steal their only fuelable source. Only 5,000 of these incredible 2D Bones are being modeled into 3D Characters for the revolutionary Abnormal Games. The first 3D skeleton to capture a brain mutates into an Abnormal Creature, unlocking a new phase in the game and a new collection. Are you ready to take part in the Broken Bones Graveyard?

How was the
collection created?

Coming Out of the Middle east, with the concept and visualization from the perspective of one Person. The Broken Bones Founder Omar (coming from an American Lebanese Background) partnered up with his best friend Karim to Create a Gaming concept over a year and half ago. This concept came up during the Covid-19 pandemic where the only way we connected across different countries was online, through Games. We came up with the principle “Not Everything that dies completely fades away”.

This concept Grew Bigger as the NFT game approached the World and Web 3.0 was becoming more accessible. We decided to Put together a beautiful team of friends and family and get started on creating our Collection down to every detail possible. Our Aim in this Project is to build the best community and most perceptive NFT concept to crawl out of the Middle East Combining Beautiful and Simple 2D PFPS with the visualization of 3D Models to participate in Our upcoming Play to Earn Game
